Help

These guidelines offer information about DataFinder. For more information about research data management in general at the University of Oxford see the University RDM website at http://www.admin.ox.ac.uk/rdm/

Types of data

DataFinder holds records for research data of many types across all disciplines. Research data defined as 'the recorded information (regardless of the form or the media in which they may exist) necessary to support or validate a research project's observations, findings or outputs.' Data might be numerical, images, audio files, survey data, or take any other form.

Search DataFinder

Click on the search tab. Searchable information includes data creator names, dataset titles, funder name, subject, Oxford Faculty and College.

Deposit a record in DataFinder

Only members of the University of Oxford are permitted to add records to DataFinder. Click on the 'contribute' tab and follow the instructions. You will need your Oxford Single-Sign-On password.
