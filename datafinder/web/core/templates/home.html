Welcome to DataFinder

Use DataFinder to find and locate research data produced by University of Oxford scholars. DataFinder will be the University of Oxford's catalogue of research data produced by members of the University. It will enable datasets to be cited and discovered whatever their location and irrespective of whether data are accessible. Development is continuing on this key strategic service. A full service will be offered once development is complete later in 2013 and dependent on funding.
